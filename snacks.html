Chin-Chin Recipe

Chin-chin is a delightful West African treat featuring bite-sized fried dough balls flavored with nutmeg and spices. This recipe offers a delicious take on this popular snack from Cameroon.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Main courses Breakfast Snacks and Desserts Sides

instructions

step 1

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, nutmeg, and cinnamon (if using).

step 2

In a separate small bowl, whisk together the softened butter, egg, and milk until well combined. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir gently with a spoon until just combined. You may need to use your hands to bring the dough together if it's too crumbly.

step 3

Lightly flour a clean work surface. Turn the dough out onto the floured surface and roll it out to a thickness of about ¼ inch. Use a knife or a pastry cutter (optional) to cut the dough into squares, diamonds, or desired shapes.

step 4

Heat enough vegetable oil in a large pot or deep fryer (if using) to a temperature of 350°F (175°C). Carefully add a few pieces of chin-chin at a time to the hot oil. Don't overcrowd the pot, as this will lower the oil temperature. Fry the chin-chin for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown. Remove the fried chin-chin with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.

step 5

Let the chin-chin cool slightly before serving. Chin-chin can be enjoyed on their own as a snack or sprinkled with powdered sugar for a touch of sweetness. Store leftover chin-chin in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days.

ingredients

3 ½ cups all-purpose flour

¾ cup sugar

½ teaspoon salt

1 ½ teaspoons baking powder

1 teaspoon ground nutmeg (freshly grated for best flavor)

¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)

½ cup unsalted butter, softened

1 large egg

¾ cup milk (or buttermilk for a tangier flavor)

Vegetable oil for deep frying

tools

Mixing bowls (large and small

Whisk

Rolling pin

Knife or pastry cutter (optional)

Spoon

Slotted spoon

Paper towels (for draining)

Deep fryer or large pot (optional)

Banana Cakes

This recipe yields a delicious and moist banana bread bursting with banana flavor. It's a perfect way to use up ripe bananas and enjoy a satisfying treat.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

60 min.

serving

10 servings

Tags : Main courses Breakfast Snacks and Desserts Sides

instructions

step 1

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour your loaf pan. You can also line the bottom of the pan with parchment paper for easier removal.

step 2

In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon (if using), and nutmeg.

step 3

In a separate bowl, using an electric mixer, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

step 4

Beat in the eggs one at a time, scraping down the sides of the bowl after each addition.

step 5

Add the mashed bananas and vanilla extract to the creamed butter mixture and mix until well combined. In two batches, alternately add the dry ingredients and buttermilk to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined after each addition.

step 6

If using, gently fold in the chopped nuts or chocolate chips with a rubber spatula.

step 7

Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean

step 8

Let the banana bread cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely. Once cool, slice and enjoy!

ingredients

1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon baking soda

½ teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)

½ teaspoon ground nutmeg (freshly grated for best flavor)

½ cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, softened

¾ cup granulated sugar

½ cup packed light brown sugar

2 large eggs

3 very ripe bananas, mashed

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

½ cup buttermilk (or milk with 1 tablespoon vinegar added)

tools

Mixing bowls (large and small)

Electric mixer (handheld or stand mixer)

Loaf pan (9x5 inch)

Whisk

Rubber spatula

Salad Recipe

This recipe offers a basic guide for creating a delicious fruit salad. Feel free to customize it with your favorite seasonal fruits!

prep time

10 min.

cook time

None required

serving

6 servings

Tags : Main courses Breakfast Snacks and Desserts Sides

instructions

step 1

Wash and dry all fruits thoroughly. Hull the strawberries, remove stems and pits from other fruits as needed. Chop larger fruits like pineapple, mango, and kiwi into bite-sized pieces. If using nuts, chop them coarsely. (Optional: Toast the nuts in a dry pan for a few minutes for extra flavor.)

step 2

In a large bowl, combine the chopped fruits, nuts (if using), and fresh mint (if using). In a small bowl, whisk together the honey and lemon juice. Pour the dressing over the fruit mixture and toss gently to coat evenly. Taste and adjust the sweetness and tartness by adding more honey or lemon juice as desired.

step 3

Refrigerate the fruit salad for at least 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Serve chilled in individual bowls.

ingredients

2 cups mixed fruits (such as strawberries, blueberries, grapes, pineapple, mango, kiwi, or any combination you prefer)

1/4 cup chopped nuts (optional, such as almonds, walnuts, or pecans)

1/4 cup chopped fresh mint (optional)

2 tablespoons honey (or to taste)

1 tablespoon lemon juice (or to taste)

tools

Cutting board

Knife

Mixing bowls (large and small)

Serving spoon
